Schmeeckle Reserve Webcam Mockup

I created a design mockup for a possible webcam section for the Schmeeckle Reserve website. The main goal of this design was to be expandable, since my thought process was that the site would eventually grow and add more webcams. Another important factor was matching the already existing Schmeeckle website in my page's aesthetics, ensuring a seamless experience between the page and the rest of the site.
