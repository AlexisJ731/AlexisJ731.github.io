✦ Overview


For this project, part of the deliverables were done solo, while the final deliverables were done in a team of three. These were done for a fictional organization called People Helping People, an organization which focused on matching voulunteer with people in the community in need of assistance with tasks such as landscaping or grocery shopping. For this project, we focused on the design of the site and making it accessable for the target users.


✦ What I Overcame


The biggest struggle for this project came from not just the design process itself, but also working together as a team. As a group, we had to find times to communicate to get our deliverables completed in a timely manner alongside our other classes.


✦ Solution


We eventually found a method of communcation that worked for all of us. At that point, we split the work evenly between the three of us and had frequent in-person and virtual communication to ensure a cohesive final presentation.
